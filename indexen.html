Version 1.7 Beta 2 [02/01/2022]

What's new?

  • Added a CEsium logo and description.
  • We've added over 100 new pokémons; each of them has its own health, attack, and defense (they had almost all the same data in version 1.6).
  • Players can now have a look at their game profile; they will be able to see some information such as their game level, experience, and the number of victories.
  • Players have also the ability to flee a battle. 
  • We've added potions in-game; to heal a pokémon, you will need to choose a healing potion (25, 50, 100, 250, 500, and full charge). Actuellement, these potions are unlimited, but in the next updates, we're planning to make them fee-based.
  • Bug fixes, as usual.

Description:

Pokémon 83 is an adaptation of Pokémon 82 on CE calculators. Discover, train and capture powerful Pokémon to show who is the best trainer. Who knows? You maybe ;)

Updates are often available. Make sure you are up to date on: http://ti-pla.net/a2788274

Enjoy!
